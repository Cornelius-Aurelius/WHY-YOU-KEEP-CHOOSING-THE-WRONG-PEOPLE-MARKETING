Why this book matters

Sometimes the painful part is not meeting the wrong person. It is recognising the pattern.

The book is built around one uncomfortable question: why can someone see the signs, feel the damage, promise themselves they are done, and still choose the same kind of person again?

Why You Keep Choosing the Wrong People is not about blaming the reader for wanting love. It is about slowing the pattern down enough to see what has been driving it.

It speaks to the person who confuses intensity with connection, accepts breadcrumbs as proof, gives too many chances, ignores red flags, or keeps hoping the wrong person will finally become the right one.

The goal is simple: help the reader protect their heart without closing it.

The book helps turn emotional confusion into clearer choices.
